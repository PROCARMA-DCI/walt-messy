Daily Preparation & Customer Greeting

Daily Preparation for Scheduled Appointments

  • Use Pre-Writes from CDK and SmartVMA.
  • Check for open recalls, special coverages, or campaigns.
  • Review Manufacturer & Spitzer Rewards.
  • Review declined or recurring services.

Customer Greeting & Engagement

  • Be prompt, enthusiastic, and professional.
  • Smile, greet the customer by name.
  • Meet the customer at their vehicle.
  • Review recalls, promotions, and declined services.
  • Request permission for MPVI and walk-around.
Repair Order & MPVI

Identify Customer’s Primary Concern

  • When does it happen?
  • How often does it occur?
  • What does it sound like?
  • High speed or low speed?
  • Hot or cold engine?
  • Constant or intermittent?

MPVI Expectations

  • Explain Green, Yellow, Red (RIM Method).
  • Customer receives a video inspection.
  • Save MPVI results in system.
SmartVMA & Warranties

SmartVMA Menu

  • Present maintenance or diagnosis plan.
  • Explain 60,000 mile service if due.
  • Confirm customer approval.

Warranties & Protection Plans

  • Review remaining factory warranty.
  • Explain Spitzer Shield LPP.
  • Provide LPP delivery sheet.
Customer Communication & Delivery

Status & Updates

  • Confirm waiter or non-waiter.
  • Update at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.
  • Present MPVI at 25% of repair time.

Active Delivery

  • Review repair order.
  • Explain MPVI results.
  • Review warranties & rebates.
  • Escort customer to vehicle.
  • Thank customer and request survey.
